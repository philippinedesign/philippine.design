philippine.design is an ongoing initiative to investigate the identity, history, & position of the Filipino designer. We plan on pursuing archival, publishing, and research to challenge the notions of how 'Filipino design' is embodied.
We're maintained by Developh, a critical technology institute in the Philippines.
